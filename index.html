Why Calling Works

When constituents call their legislators, it creates a tangible record of public opinion that can influence policy decisions. Legislative offices track constituent communications, and even just a few calls on an issue can make a difference in how a legislator votes.

Personal stories and local impacts are especially powerful when communicating with your legislators.
