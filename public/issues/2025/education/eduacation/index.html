Key Issues

Education

Education is a cornerstone issue in Indiana's legislative agenda. The state has been working to improve its education system through various initiatives, including increasing funding for K-12 schools, implementing new programs to help students succeed, and addressing teacher compensation. Recent focus areas include literacy, workforce development programs, school choice options, and enhanced teacher recruitment and retention strategies.

Call Script

When calling your representative about this issue, consider using the following script:

Hello, my name is [Your Name] and I’m a constituent from [Your City/Town].

I’m calling to express my support for increased education funding and better teacher compensation in Indiana. Specifically, I encourage support for HB 1001 and SB 223.

Strong public education is vital for our state’s future, and I believe we need to invest more in our schools and educators.

Can I count on [Representative Name] to support these education initiatives?

Thank you for your time and consideration.

Tip: Personalize your call by sharing how this issue impacts you, your family, or your community.
